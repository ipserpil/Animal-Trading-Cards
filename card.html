Chamois

Can jump almost 2 m in height, 6 m in length and can run at speeds of 50 km/h on uneven ground.

  • Scientific name: Rupicapra rupicapra;
  • Habitat: Alpine and sub alpine meadows;
  • Average weight: From 30 to 60 kg;
  • Diet: Herbs, flowers, lichens, mosses and young pine shoots.

Chamois fur is a chestnut color. It can be found at elevations up to at least 3,600 m, in both relatively steep and flatter terrain. The range of the chamois, includes Alps, Pyrenees, Carpathians, Tatra Mountains, Balkans, parts of Turkey, Caucasus, Apennines and New Zealand.
